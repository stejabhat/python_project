Rich Heritage Of India


Indian heritage is the richness of Indian art, architecture, classical dance, music, flora, and wildlife, as well as the ingrained secular mindset of its people1.

India has 42 UNESCO World Heritage Sites that showcase its cultural and natural diversity.

Some of the most famous heritage sites in India are the Taj Mahal, Khajuraho, Hampi, Ajanta Caves, Ellora Caves, Bodh Gaya, Sun Temple, and Red Fort Complex.
